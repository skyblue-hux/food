Mutton

Disclaimer: This recipe is taken straight from an old Elizabethan recipe book which was extremely simplistic as no quantities were described inside.

Instructions

  1. Boil neck of mutton in water and salt.

  2. For sauce:

  3. Boil samphire, broth, verjuice, large mace, pepper, onion, yolks of hard eggs minced, sweet herbs, and salt together for half an hour or more.
  4. Beat dish up with butter and limon.
  5. Dish meat upon sippets and pour it on.
  6. Garnish dish with hard whites of eggs and parsley minced together with sliced lemon.
  7. Dress a leg or breast of mutton with it.

Recipe
