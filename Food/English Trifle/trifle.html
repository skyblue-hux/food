English Trifle

Instructions

  1. Prepare the yellow cake according to package directions using a 9-inch cake pan (reserve one layer for another use).
  2. *One baked cake layer horizontally cut into two equal layers.*
  3. Place one sliced layer into a trifle dish or a large glass bowl with straight sides; cover with 1/2 cup jam and half of the sliced peaches.
  4. Pour approximately half of the reserved peach juice over the cake or until it is moist but not sloppy.
  5. Pour 1/4 cup sherry or brandy over the top, as evenly as possible.
  6. Place the second sliced cake layer on top and cover with remaining jam and peaches; pour remaining peach juice and sherry or brandy over the top.
  7. Prepare the vanilla pudding according to package directions, using the 3 cups of milk.
  8. Pour hot pudding over the top of the cake; let cool.
  9. When cool, spread with whipped cream.
  10. Decorate with toasted sliced almonds.
  11. Cover with plastic wrap and refrigerate overnight.

Recipe

  • 1 (18 1/4-ounce) package yellow cake mix
  • 1 cup raspberry jam, divided
  • 1 (29-ounce) can peaches, sliced and drained (reserve juice)
  • 1/2 cup sherry or brandy, divided (optional)
  • 1 (6-serving size) vanilla pudding mix (not instant)
  • 3 cups milk
  • 2 cups whipping cream, sweetened
  • Almonds, sliced and toasted
