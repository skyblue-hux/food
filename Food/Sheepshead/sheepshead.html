Sheepshead

Instructions

  1. Heat up the grill to 350-400 degrees.
  2. Dry the filets.
  3. Cover them with olive oil in the pan.
  4. Season the upside of the filets.
  5. Put the filets on the grill seasoned side down and season the other side.
  6. Flip them after 2-3 minutes per side, depending on how thick they are.
  7. All done! Simple right!

Recipe

  • Sheepshead filets
  • Seasoning (of your choice)
  • Olive oil
