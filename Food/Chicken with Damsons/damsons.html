Chicken with Damsons

Instructions

  1. Mix flour, butter, yolks, and saffron together.
  2. Wash, cut and pre-cook chicken.
  3. Put a bit of butter on your baking dish.
  4. Roll out and put the pastry in your baking dish.
  5. Add the chicken, damsons, spices, butter, and raisins.

Recipe

  • Filling:

  • 4 chicken breasts
  • 20 damsons
  • Butter - the size of a walnut
  • One teaspoon cloves
  • One teaspoon mace
  • Two teaspoon cinnamon
  • One teaspoon sugar
  • Handful of currants or raisins

  • Pastry:

  • Flour for dough
  • 12 egg yolks
  • Little saffron for coloring
