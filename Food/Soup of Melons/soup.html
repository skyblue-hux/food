Melon Soup

Instructions

  1. Remove the peel and seeds inside of the melon.
  2. In the pan, melt chicken fat and butter to be spread all over the inside of the pot.
  3. Cut the melon into slices tiny enough to fit in your mouth, then place in a pot to cook.
  4. Ater it has cooked, place it in a strainer to ensure that there are no more seeds left in the melon.
  5. Replace the pot with a bit of broth, gooseberries, or whole unripe grapes to let boil.
  6. After it has sat for a short time (very short), place beaten eggs and grated cheese inside the pot.
  7. You can also cook the melon with broth and open it to mix in the eggs, cheese and spices.
  8. Mix in gooseberries, grapes and verjuice.

Recipe

  • Melon
  • Butter or chicken fat
  • Grated cheese
  • Eggs
  • Verjuice or gooseberries
